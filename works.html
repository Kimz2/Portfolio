Family Flowers is an online floral business based in Atlanta, Georgia. We partnered with them to develop Bokay: a standalone online application where users create custom floral arrangements with the same Family Flowers standard of exceptional quality and service.

Mentalhealth.gov provides information and support to Americans on the topic of mental health. Our primary goal with the redesign was to convey the philosophy that Mental Health Matters. We wanted to deviate from traditional government websites by creating a welcoming atmosphere that evokes lightness and simplicity.

Adopastar.org is a nonprofit dedicated to raising funds for scientific research in the field of astronomy. Our aim was to redesign a contemporary interface that was minimal and futuristic. We wanted to create an engaging experience that provided incentive by piquing the interest of users who are unfamiliar with astronomy.
